NJL2L Program Overview — Key Requirements & Rules
📋 Registration & Documents
  • District registers resident at NJL2L.org with job description, Statement of Assurance, and Board minutes
  • Registration fee: $950 (as of Jul 1, 2024)
  • 5 state-required documents must be completed within 2 weeks of pairing
  • Residency Plan required for both Year 1 & Year 2
  • Resume must be attached to Statement of Assurance
📝 Formative Assessments
  • 4 formative assessments: end of Months 5, 10, 15, and 20
  • Based on PSEL standards, mentor observations, peer group, portfolio, and time requirements
  • Mentor holds formal conference with resident after each formative
  • Confidential — cannot be shared with district without resident's written consent
🎓 Summative Assessment
  • Completed by mentor at end of Month 23
  • Includes verification of all requirements, Action Research review, and professional growth rating
  • 3 possible outcomes: Approved, Insufficient Progress, or Disapproved
  • Submitted to NJL2L within 2 weeks of mentor/resident conference
  • Transmitted to NJDOE by NJL2L
🔬 Action Research Project
  • Proposal submitted to mentor by end of Year 1 for approval
  • Job-embedded, PSEL-focused project
  • Resident may only begin after mentor approval
  • Must be complete before Month 23 Summative Assessment
  • Presented via PowerPoint to Peer Support Group at end of Year 2
👥 Peer Support Groups
  • Organized regionally/by county across NJ
  • Monthly meetings — attendance is mandatory and documented
  • "Team mentoring" approach with mentor facilitating
  • Hours count toward total residency hour requirement
⚠️ Interruption Rules
  • District change or position change: residency continues with same mentor
  • District-approved leave: residency put on hold, no fees returned
  • Leave > 2 years: must restart the entire program
  • Dismissed from position: no fees returned unless requested
  • Interim and Acting positions are ineligible for NJL2L
